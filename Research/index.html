Experimental Course Management System

Also, from April to June of 2017 I took part in a project about Online Course Experiment System. My main responsibility included conceiving the general and detailed design of the system, developing the Spring+Hibernate+Maven framework, designing the front-end interface with the combination of HTML, CSS and JSP, programming control codes, and maintaining and operating of databases. Mathematical Contest in Modeling

In January of 2017, I worked with two students to take part in Mathematical Contest in Modeling of the United States. Our task was to investigate how to realize intelligent urbanization. Two cities were chosen, Yushu and Nottingham. We obtained their planning facts from the google map and digitalized the remote sensing image. We employed principal component analysis approach to select ten indicators which could best predict the development of the city. We modeled with ten formulas to calculate each indicator. The final mathematic model was a combination of ten indicators with different weight. We collected the data of Shenzhen City from 1990 to 2000, in order to simulate ten-year development path and compare with the actual situations. The results showed that the deviation was less than 10%, demonstrating that our model was valid and stable. In the end, our team was awarded honorable mention title。The competition experience taught me how to build models and exercised my teamwork abilities.
